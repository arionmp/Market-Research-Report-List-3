<p><p>Engine remapping services refer to the modification of the engine control unit (ECU) in a vehicle to enhance performance, fuel efficiency, or other aspects of engine operation. OBD remapping involves making changes to the ECU settings through the vehicle's onboard diagnostic port. ECU remapping, on the other hand, involves directly accessing and modifying the ECU's software. Other types of engine remapping services may include chip tuning or tuning box installations, which offer different approaches to optimizing engine performance.</p></p>
