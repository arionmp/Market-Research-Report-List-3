<p><strong>The Wire and Cable Recycling Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Peeling</li><li>Mechanical Peeling</li><li>Mechanical Crushing</li><li>Chemical Method</li><li>Freezing</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wire and cable recycling market types include manual peeling, where outer insulation is manually removed; mechanical peeling, using machines to strip the insulation; mechanical crushing, which crushes cables to separate metal and plastic; chemical method, involving the use of chemicals to dissolve the insulation; and freezing, where cables are frozen to make the insulation brittle for easy removal. Each method offers a different approach to recycling wire and cable, with varying levels of efficiency and environmental impact.</p></p>
