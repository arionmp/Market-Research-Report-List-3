<p><p>Resistive strain gauges are used to measure the deformation or strain in an object when a mechanical force is applied. The market types include digital type and pointer type strain gauges. Digital strain gauges provide digital readings of the strain measured, making it easier for users to record and analyze data. On the other hand, pointer type strain gauges display the strain measurement through a moving pointer on a dial, providing a visual indication of the strain. Both types serve the same purpose but cater to different user preferences.</p></p>
