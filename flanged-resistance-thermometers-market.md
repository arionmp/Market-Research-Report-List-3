<p><strong>The Flanged Resistance Thermometers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>2 Wire</li><li>3 Wire</li><li>4 Wire</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Flanged resistance thermometers come in different market types such as 2-wire, 3-wire, and 4-wire configurations. The 2-wire system is the simplest design but can introduce errors due to lead resistance. The 3-wire system compensates for lead resistance and provides more accurate temperature measurement. The 4-wire system offers the highest level of accuracy by eliminating lead resistance entirely. Each system caters to different needs and requirements, allowing users to choose the best option based on their specific application.</p></p>
