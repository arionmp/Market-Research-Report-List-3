<p><p>Mixed current spray drying equipment market includes various types such as rotary atomizer, nozzle atomizer, fluidized, closed loop, centrifugal, and others. Rotary atomizers are known for their high-speed rotation and fine droplet formation. Nozzle atomizers use pressure to create a spray. Fluidized spray dryers have a bed of particles fluidized by hot air. Closed loop systems recycle drying gas, reducing energy consumption. Centrifugal dryers utilize centrifugal force for atomization. Other types may include custom variations or combinations of these methods.</p></p>
