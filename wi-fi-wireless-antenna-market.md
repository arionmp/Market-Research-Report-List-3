<p><ul><li>Omni directional antennas</li><li>Directional Antennas</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wi-Fi Wireless Antenna markets are generally divided into two types based on their design and functionality. Omni directional antennas transmit and receive signals in all directions, making them suitable for providing coverage in all directions from a central point. On the other hand, Directional antennas focus their signal in one specific direction, making them ideal for point-to-point communication or extending the range in a specific direction. Both types of antennas play a crucial role in extending Wi-Fi coverage in different scenarios.</p></p>
