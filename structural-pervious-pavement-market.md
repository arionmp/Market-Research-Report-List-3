<p><p>Structural Pervious Pavement offers three main types - Pervious Concrete, Porous Asphalt, and Interlocking Concrete Pavers. Pervious Concrete is a durable option made of cement and aggregates, allowing water to pass through. Porous Asphalt consists of open-graded materials that allow water infiltration. Interlocking Concrete Pavers are durable, stable units that help with stormwater management. These types of structural pervious pavement options provide sustainable solutions for managing stormwater runoff and reducing the impact of urban development on the environment.</p></p>
