Open systems allow for continuous inflow and outflow of water during the procedure, providing more control and comfort for the user. Closed systems, on the other hand, involve a single-use disposable speculum and tubing, offering a more hygienic and self-administered option. Both types cater to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
