<p><p>Engineered flooring market is divided into two main types: three layers type and multilayer type. Three layers type consists of three layers of wood stacked on top of each other, providing stability and resistance to moisture and temperature changes. On the other hand, multilayer type consists of multiple layers of wood bonded together, offering even greater durability and strength. Both types are popular choices for consumers seeking high-quality, long-lasting flooring options with a variety of finishes and styles.</p></p>
