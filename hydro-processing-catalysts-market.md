<p><p>Hydro-processing catalysts are widely used in the petroleum refining industry to remove impurities and convert heavy hydrocarbons into lighter, more valuable products. The two main types of hydro-processing catalysts are hydrotreating and hydrocracking. Hydrotreating catalysts are primarily used to remove sulfur, nitrogen, and other impurities from crude oil, while hydrocracking catalysts are used to break down heavy hydrocarbons into lighter, more valuable products such as gasoline and diesel. Both types of catalysts play a critical role in improving the efficiency and quality of refined petroleum products.</p></p>
