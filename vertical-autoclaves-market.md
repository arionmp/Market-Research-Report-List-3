<p><p>Vertical autoclaves are commonly available in two types: Fully automatic and semi-automatic. Fully automatic vertical autoclaves are equipped with advanced features such as touch screen control panels, pre-programmed sterilization cycles, and automatic door locking mechanisms. These autoclaves require minimal manual intervention and offer efficient and hassle-free sterilization process. On the other hand, semi-automatic vertical autoclaves require some manual operation for tasks such as loading and unloading materials and setting sterilization parameters. Both types are widely used in laboratories, hospitals, and research facilities for sterilization of equipment and media.</p></p>
