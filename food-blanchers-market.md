<p><p>Food blanchers are used in the food processing industry to heat food products quickly and efficiently before further processing. Three common types of food blanchers include belt blanchers, drum blanchers, and screw blanchers. Belt blanchers use a conveyor belt system to pass food products through a hot water or steam bath. Drum blanchers rotate the food products in a heated drum to ensure even heating. Screw blanchers use a screw mechanism to mix and heat the food products. Each type of blancher offers unique advantages for different food processing applications.</p></p>
