<p><p>Automated refuse collection systems come in two main types: Gravity System and Full Vacuum System. Gravity System uses gravity to transport waste through underground pipes to a central collection point, while Full Vacuum System relies on a vacuum pump system to suction waste from individual collection points. Both systems offer efficient and hygienic waste disposal solutions, with Gravity System being more cost-effective for smaller areas and Full Vacuum System being suitable for larger, more complex environments.</p></p>
