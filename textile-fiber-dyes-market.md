<p><ul><li>Vinylsulphone Dye (VS)</li><li>Monochlorotriazine Dye (MCT)</li><li>Bi-functional Dyes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Textile fiber dyes are classified into various types based on their chemical structure and properties. Vinylsulphone Dyes (VS) are highly reactive dyes that provide excellent color fastness and brightness. Monochlorotriazine Dyes (MCT) are another type of reactive dyes known for their good color yield and resistance to washing and light. Bi-functional dyes are a type of dye that contain two reactive groups, allowing for a stronger bond with the fabric and resulting in improved color fastness. These dyes are widely used in the textile industry for dyeing various fibers.</p></p>
