<p><p>Tissue flotation baths are commonly available in two main types: round and rectangular. Round tissue flotation baths are compact and ideal for small laboratories or limited bench space. They offer uniform temperature distribution to ensure accurate tissue processing. On the other hand, rectangular tissue flotation baths are larger in size and can accommodate multiple tissue samples at once. They are suitable for larger laboratories with higher sample volumes. Both types are essential for histology and pathology labs for the optimal processing of tissue samples.</p></p>
