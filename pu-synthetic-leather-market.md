<p><p>PU Synthetic Leather is categorized into two main types, Normal PU Leather and Microfiber PU Leather. Normal PU Leather is made from polyurethane coating on a base material of split leather, giving it a softer texture and a more natural look. On the other hand, Microfiber PU Leather is made from microfiber fabric coated with polyurethane, offering a more durable and water-resistant option. Both types of PU Synthetic Leather are commonly used in fashion accessories, upholstery, and automotive interiors.</p></p>
