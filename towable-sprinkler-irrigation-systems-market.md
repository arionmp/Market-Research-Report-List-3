<p><p>The towable sprinkler irrigation systems market includes various types such as center pivot, lateral move, solid set, and others. Center pivot systems consist of a long water pipe attached to a pivot point, rotating in a circular motion. Lateral move systems move along a linear path, covering a larger area. Solid set systems are stationary and consist of individual sprinklers set up in a specific pattern. Other types may include traveler, wheel line, or hose reel systems, offering flexibility in irrigation methods.</p></p>
