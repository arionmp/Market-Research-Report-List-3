<p><strong>The Polyurethane Amine Catalyst Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Reactive Amine Catalysts</li><li>Non-reactive Amine Catalysts</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polyurethane amine catalyst market can be divided into two main types: reactive amine catalysts and non-reactive amine catalysts. Reactive amine catalysts actively participate in the polymerization reaction, facilitating the formation of polyurethane. On the other hand, non-reactive amine catalysts do not take part in the reaction but instead act as stabilizers or modifiers to enhance the performance of the end product. Both types of catalysts play crucial roles in the production of polyurethane materials for various applications.</p></p>
