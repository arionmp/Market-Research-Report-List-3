<p><p>Diode Medical Laser market consists of three main types: Electric Injection Laser, Light Pump Laser, and High Energy Electron Beam Excitation Laser. Electric Injection Laser uses electrical current to stimulate light emission, Light Pump Laser uses another laser to pump the diode, and High Energy Electron Beam Excitation Laser uses high energy electron beams to excite the diode. Each type offers unique benefits and applications in medical procedures, such as surgery, dermatology, and ophthalmology, making them essential tools in the healthcare industry.</p></p>
