<p><p>Wireless humidity transmitters come in different market types based on the number of channels they can monitor. Single channel transmitters are designed to monitor humidity in a single location, making them ideal for small spaces. Dual channel transmitters can monitor humidity in two separate locations simultaneously, providing more flexibility for larger areas. Multi-channel transmitters can monitor humidity in multiple locations, making them suitable for complex environments where data from various points is required for accurate monitoring and control.</p></p>
