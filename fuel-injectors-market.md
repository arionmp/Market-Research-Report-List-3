<p><strong>The Fuel Injectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multiport Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Fuel injectors are used in different types of fuel systems in vehicles. Throttle Body Injection (TBI) is a single-point injection system where fuel is injected directly into the throttle body. Multiport Injection involves injecting fuel into each intake port of the engine for more precise fuel delivery. Direct Injection sprays fuel directly into the combustion chamber of each cylinder, leading to improved fuel efficiency and performance. These different types of fuel injection systems cater to various vehicle requirements and have their own advantages.</p></p>
