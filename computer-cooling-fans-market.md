<p><strong>The Computer Cooling Fans Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Axial Fans</li><li>Centrifugal Fans</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Computer cooling fans market can be categorized into two main types: Axial Fans and Centrifugal Fans. Axial fans operate by moving air parallel to the fan's axis, providing efficient cooling for systems with high airflow requirements. On the other hand, Centrifugal fans work by directing air perpendicular to the fan's axis, making them suitable for delivering high pressure airflow. Both types of fans play a crucial role in maintaining optimal temperatures in computer systems, ensuring efficient performance and preventing overheating.</p></p>
