<p><p>Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts are classified into two main types: Strand Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts and Layer Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts. Strand Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts consist of single strands of steel cords running the length of the belt, providing high tensile strength. Layer Steel Cord Rubber Conveyor Belts, on the other hand, have multiple layers of steel cords embedded within the rubber cover, offering greater flexibility and impact resistance. Both types are widely used in industries for efficient material handling and conveying.</p></p>
