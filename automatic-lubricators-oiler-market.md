<p><p>Automatic lubricators or oilers provide constant lubrication to machinery to ensure smooth operation and prevent wear and tear. There are three main types of automatic lubricators: Oil Mist Type, Single Point Type, and Multipoint Type. Oil Mist Type lubricators disperse a fine mist of oil throughout the machinery, Single Point Type lubricators deliver lubrication to a specific point, and Multipoint Type lubricators distribute oil to multiple points. Each type caters to different machinery needs and maintenance requirements.</p></p>
