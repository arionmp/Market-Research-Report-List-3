<p><ul><li>Flat Bottom</li><li>U-Bottom</li><li>V-Bottom</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Cell culture microplates come in various types based on the bottom shape, including flat bottom, U-bottom, and V-bottom. Flat bottom microplates facilitate easy cell attachment and spreading, making them ideal for cell growth and imaging analysis. U-bottom microplates are designed for uniform cell sedimentation and are commonly used for cell suspension assays. V-bottom microplates are suitable for small volume applications and promote optimal cell culture conditions. Each type of microplate offers unique advantages depending on the specific cell culture requirements.</p></p>
