<p><p>Ultra High Pressure Seals are used in various industries and can be classified into two main types - Static Seals and Dynamic Seals. Static Seals are used to prevent the leakage of fluids between stationary components, while Dynamic Seals are used to prevent the leakage of fluids between moving components. Static Seals are commonly found in applications such as pipelines and valves, while Dynamic Seals are used in applications such as hydraulic cylinders and pumps. Both types play a crucial role in maintaining the integrity of high-pressure systems.</p></p>
