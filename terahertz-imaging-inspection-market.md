<p><p>Terahertz imaging inspection market consists of two main types: passive terahertz imaging and active terahertz imaging. Passive terahertz imaging utilizes natural terahertz radiation emitted by objects to create images for inspection purposes. On the other hand, active terahertz imaging involves the use of external terahertz sources to illuminate objects and create images. Both types of imaging techniques have their own advantages and applications in various industries such as security, healthcare, and material testing.</p></p>
