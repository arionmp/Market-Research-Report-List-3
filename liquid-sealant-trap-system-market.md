<p><p>The liquid sealant trap system market consists of two main types: slug traps and sealing traps. Slug traps are designed to capture large quantities of liquid sealant, preventing overflow and spillage. Sealing traps, on the other hand, are used to create a tight seal around pipes or other surfaces to prevent leaks and maintain pressure. Both types of traps are crucial in ensuring the efficient and safe use of liquid sealants in various industries.</p></p>
