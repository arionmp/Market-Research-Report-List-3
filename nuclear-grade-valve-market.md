<p><p>Nuclear grade valves are essential components in nuclear power plants for controlling the flow of fluids and gases. The main types of valves used in the nuclear industry include gate valves, globe valves, butterfly valves, ball valves, check valves, diaphragm valves, and other specialized valves. Gate valves are used for on/off control, globe valves for regulating flow, butterfly valves for quick shut-off, ball valves for tight shut-off, check valves for preventing backflow, and diaphragm valves for precise control. Each type serves a specific function to ensure the safe and efficient operation of nuclear power plants.</p></p>
