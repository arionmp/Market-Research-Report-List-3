<p><p>Automotive connectors are categorized into three main types: Wire to Wire Connector, Wire to Board Connector, and Board to Board Connector. Wire to Wire Connectors are used to connect two wires, Wire to Board Connectors connect wires to circuit boards, and Board to Board Connectors connect two circuit boards. Each type of connector serves a specific purpose in automotive applications, providing secure and reliable electrical connections essential for various components to function properly in vehicles.</p></p>
