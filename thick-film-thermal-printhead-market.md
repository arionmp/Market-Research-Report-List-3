<p><p>The thick film thermal printhead market includes two main types: corner type and side face type. Corner type printheads are designed with the heating elements located at the corners of the printhead, allowing for efficient heat distribution and printing accuracy. Side face type printheads have the heating elements aligned along the side faces of the printhead, providing consistent heat transfer and high-resolution printing capabilities. Both types cater to specific printing needs and preferences, offering varying levels of performance and durability in thermal printing applications.</p></p>
