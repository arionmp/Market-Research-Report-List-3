<p><strong>The Refractive Modulator Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Refractive modulator market can be categorized into Type I and Type II markets based on their operating principles. Type I modulators utilize the change in refractive index of the material to modulate light, while Type II modulators rely on the change in absorption properties. Type I modulators are known for their high efficiency and fast response times, making them suitable for high-speed applications. On the other hand, Type II modulators are preferred for their simplicity and lower cost, making them suitable for cost-sensitive applications.</p></p>
