<p><p>Plasma etching agents market is divided into two main types: wet etching agents and dry etching agents. Wet etching agents involve the removal of material through immersion in a liquid solution, while dry etching agents use gas-phase reactions to etch materials. Wet etching agents are commonly used for isotropic etching processes, while dry etching agents are preferred for anisotropic etching. Both types of etching agents play crucial roles in semiconductor manufacturing and microfabrication processes, offering distinct advantages and disadvantages based on specific application requirements.</p></p>
