<p><p>Etched grating market includes two main types: transmission grating and reflection grating. Transmission grating allows light to pass through, dispersing it into different directions, commonly used in spectrometers and optical communication systems. Reflection grating, on the other hand, reflects light at specific angles, often used in laser systems and astronomy. Both types of gratings are crucial components in various industries for manipulating and controlling light waves for different applications.</p></p>
