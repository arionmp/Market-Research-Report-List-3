<p><p>Industrial grade HPMC market is segmented into three types based on viscosity levels: low viscosity, middle viscosity, and high viscosity. Low viscosity HPMC is ideal for applications requiring easier dispersion and faster dissolution. Middle viscosity HPMC is commonly used in construction materials such as cement, mortar, and tile adhesives. High viscosity HPMC is suitable for industries requiring thickening, binding, and water retention properties, such as pharmaceuticals, personal care products, and food processing. Each type offers specific qualities to meet different industrial needs.</p></p>
