Current Transformers are used to measure electric currents in power systems by stepping down the current for measurements or protection purposes. Potential Transformers are used to measure voltage in power systems by stepping down the voltage for accurate readings or protective relaying. Both types of transformers play a critical role in ensuring the safe and efficient operation of indoor power systems.</p></p>
