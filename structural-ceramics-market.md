<p><p>Structural Ceramics market can be broadly classified into two main types: Oxide Ceramics and Non-Oxide Ceramics. Oxide Ceramics are made from compounds containing oxygen and a metal, such as alumina or zirconia, and are known for their high strength, thermal stability, and corrosion resistance. Non-Oxide Ceramics, on the other hand, are made from compounds that do not contain oxygen, such as carbides, nitrides, or borides, and are valued for their extreme hardness and wear resistance. Both types of ceramics play a crucial role in various industries due to their unique properties and applications.</p></p>
