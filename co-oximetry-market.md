<p><p>Co-oximetry is a technique used to measure the levels of different forms of hemoglobin in the blood. There are two main types of Co-oximetry markets: Transmission Type and Reflectance Type. Transmission Type utilizes a light source that passes through the blood sample to analyze hemoglobin levels. Reflectance Type, on the other hand, uses a light source that reflects off the blood sample to measure hemoglobin levels. Both types of markets have their strengths and weaknesses, but both are valuable tools in diagnosing and monitoring certain medical conditions.</p></p>
