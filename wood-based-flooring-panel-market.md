<p><p>Wood based flooring panel market is segmented into Particleboard (PB), Medium density fibreboard (MDF), High density fibreboard (HDF), Oriented strand board (OSB), and Plywood. PB is made from wood particles bonded together, MDF is made from wood fibers compressed together, HDF is made from wood fibers compressed under high pressure, OSB is made from compressed wood strands arranged in layers, and Plywood is made from thin layers of wood veneer glued together. Each type offers different levels of durability, strength, and cost, catering to varying consumer needs.</p></p>
