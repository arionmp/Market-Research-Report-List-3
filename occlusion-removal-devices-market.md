<p><p>Occlusion removal devices are used in the treatment of ischemic strokes to remove blood clots from blocked vessels in the brain. Balloon occlusion devices are inserted into the blood vessel and inflated to block blood flow, allowing for clot retrieval. Stent retrievers are mesh-like devices that capture and remove clots. Coil retrievers use coils to ensnare and remove clots. Suction and aspiration devices use vacuum pressure to draw out clots from the blood vessel. These devices help restore blood flow and prevent further brain damage.</p></p>
