<p><strong>The Road Profile Laser Sensors Market Industry Research by Application is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Longitudinal Profile</li><li>Transverse Profile</li><li>Side Projections</li><li>Macro Texture</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Road profile laser sensors are used in various applications such as longitudinal profile measurement, transverse profile measurement, side projections, macro texture measurement, and others. Longitudinal profile measurement involves capturing the changes in elevation along the length of the road, while transverse profile measurement captures changes in elevation across the road. Side projections measure the height and width of objects on the road, while macro texture measurement assesses the road surface roughness. Other applications include detecting cracks, potholes, and rutting on road surfaces.</p></p>
