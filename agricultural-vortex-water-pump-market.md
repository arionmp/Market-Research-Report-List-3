<p><ul><li>Closed Vortex Pump</li><li>Open Vortex Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Agricultural vortex water pump market consists of two main types: closed vortex pump and open vortex pump. Closed vortex pumps are designed with a circular pump casing that creates a fluid vortex to increase efficiency and reduce wear on the pump. On the other hand, open vortex pumps have an open impeller that allows for larger solids to pass through without clogging. Both types are commonly used in agricultural irrigation systems to effectively move water from a water source to crops or fields.</p></p>
