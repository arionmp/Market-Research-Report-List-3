<p><p>Plasma surface treatment machines for leather are available in two main types: atmospheric plasma and vacuum plasma. Atmospheric plasma machines utilize ionized gas at ambient pressure to modify the surface of leather, offering a cost-effective solution for large-scale production. On the other hand, vacuum plasma machines operate at low pressure environments, making them suitable for more delicate or heat-sensitive materials. Both types of plasma treatment offer unique benefits and can be chosen based on the specific requirements of the leather manufacturing process.</p></p>
