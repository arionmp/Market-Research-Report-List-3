<p><strong>The LAN Switch Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Layer 2 Switching</li><li>Layer 3 Switching</li><li>Layer 4 Switching</li><li>Multi-Layer switching (MLS)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Layer 2 switching in the LAN switch market involves switching based on MAC addresses. Layer 3 switching involves making decisions based on IP addresses. Layer 4 switching involves making decisions based on port numbers within the transport layer. Multi-Layer switching (MLS) combines Layer 2, 3, and 4 switching to provide a more advanced and efficient switching solution for networks. Each type of switching offers different functionalities and benefits, catering to the diverse needs of various network environments.</p></p>
