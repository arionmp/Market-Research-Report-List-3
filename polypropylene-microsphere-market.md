<p><ul><li>HollowMicrosphere</li><li>SolidMicrosphere</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Polypropylene microspheres are tiny beads used in various industries for their lightweight and durable properties. There are two main types of polypropylene microspheres: Hollow microspheres and Solid microspheres. Hollow microspheres are filled with gas or vacuum inside, making them buoyant and ideal for applications such as insulation and composite materials. Solid microspheres are compact and dense, making them suitable for applications like paints, coatings, and adhesives where strength and durability are critical. Both types offer unique benefits depending on the specific needs of the market.</p></p>
