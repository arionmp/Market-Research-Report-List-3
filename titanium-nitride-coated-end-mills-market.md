<p><p>Titanium Nitride Coated End Mills are commonly classified into three types based on the cutting edge design: Peripheral Cutting Edge, End Cutting Edge, and Others. Peripheral Cutting Edge end mills have cutting edges along the side of the tool, allowing for efficient material removal around the edges of the workpiece. End Cutting Edge end mills have cutting edges at the tip of the tool, ideal for plunging and slotting applications. Other types of end mills may include unique designs or combinations of cutting edges for specialized machining purposes.</p></p>
