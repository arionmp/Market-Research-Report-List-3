<p><p>Tritium batteries come in two main types - thermal conversion batteries and non-thermal conversion batteries. Thermal conversion batteries generate energy through the heat produced by the radioactive decay of tritium, while non-thermal conversion batteries produce electricity directly from the electrons emitted during the decay process. Each type has unique advantages and applications, with thermal conversion batteries being more suitable for high-power requirements and non-thermal conversion batteries being more flexible and efficient for various electronic devices.</p></p>
