<p><p>The optical modulator market is divided into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I modulators are characterized by their high optical power handling capability and low insertion loss, making them suitable for high-speed communication applications. On the other hand, Type II modulators are known for their high extinction ratio and low driving voltage, making them more suitable for sensing and instrumentation applications.
