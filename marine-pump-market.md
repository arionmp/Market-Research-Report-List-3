<p><strong>The Marine Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Positive Displacement Pump</li><li>Roto-Dynamic Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Marine pump market comprises of two main types: Positive Displacement Pump and Roto-Dynamic Pump. Positive Displacement Pumps operate by trapping a fixed amount of fluid and forcing it into a discharge pipe, while Roto-Dynamic Pumps use centrifugal force to transfer fluid. Positive Displacement Pumps are suitable for handling viscous fluids and high-pressure applications, while Roto-Dynamic Pumps are preferred for high flow rate and low viscosity applications. Both pumps play a vital role in various marine applications, providing efficient fluid transfer solutions.</p></p>
