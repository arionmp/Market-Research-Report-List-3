<p><ul><li>Static</li><li>Dynamic</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aircraft seals market can be divided into two main types: static seals and dynamic seals. Static seals are used to prevent the passage of fluids or gases in stationary parts of the aircraft, such as doors or windows. Dynamic seals, on the other hand, are used in moving parts of the aircraft, such as landing gear or actuators, to prevent leakage of fluids or gases while allowing for movement. Both types of seals play a crucial role in ensuring the safety and efficiency of aircraft operations.</p></p>
