<p><strong>The Parachute Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Round</li><li>Cruciform</li><li>Annular and pull down apex</li><li>Rogallo wing</li><li>Ribbon and Ring</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Parachute market types vary based on their shape and design. Round parachutes are traditional and commonly used for military and cargo deployments. Cruciform parachutes have a cross-like shape, providing higher stability and control. Annular parachutes have a circular canopy with a hole in the center for air to pass through, allowing for faster descent. Pull down apex parachutes feature a unique design with a single apex point for controlled deployment. Rogallo wing parachutes have a delta-shaped design, offering maneuverability and precision landing. Ribbon and Ring parachutes consist of two separate parts, providing stability and ease of deployment.</p></p>
