<p><strong>The Transit Time Flow Meters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>With One Pair of Transducers</li><li>With Pairs of Transducers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Transit time flow meters with one pair of transducers use ultrasonic technology to measure the difference in time it takes for an ultrasonic signal to travel with and against the flow of liquid in a pipe. This type of flow meter is ideal for single pipe systems. On the other hand, transit time flow meters with pairs of transducers use multiple transducers to measure flow in pipes with varying sizes and complex geometries. This type of flow meter is suitable for large industrial applications.</p></p>
