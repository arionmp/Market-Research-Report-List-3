<p><strong>The Anti-Fungal Agents Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Azoles</li><li>Polyenes</li><li>5-fluorocytosine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Anti-fungal agents are classified into three main types: Azoles, Polyenes, and 5-fluorocytosine. Azoles work by inhibiting the synthesis of ergosterol, an essential component of the fungal cell membrane. Polyenes work by binding to ergosterol, disrupting the integrity of the fungal cell membrane. 5-fluorocytosine interferes with fungal RNA and protein synthesis. These agents are used to treat a variety of fungal infections and are available in the market in various forms such as creams, ointments, and oral medications.</p></p>
