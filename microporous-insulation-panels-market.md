<p><p>Microporous insulation panels are categorized into two main types: Flexible microporous insulation panels and Rigid microporous insulation panels. Flexible panels are designed to be easily molded and shaped, making them suitable for irregular surfaces or intricate applications. Rigid panels, on the other hand, are more solid and structured, providing excellent thermal insulation properties for structural components or machinery. Both types offer high thermal efficiency and are used in various industries such as construction, automotive, and aerospace for their excellent insulating properties.</p></p>
