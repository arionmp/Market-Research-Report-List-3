There are three main types of DROs systems based on the number of axes they measure: 2-axis, 3-axis, and 4-axis. </p><p>2-axis DROs measure movement along two axes, typically X and Y. </p><p>3-axis DROs add the capability to measure movement along a third axis, usually Z. </p><p>4-axis DROs can measure movement along a fourth axis, allowing for even more complex machining operations.</p></p>
