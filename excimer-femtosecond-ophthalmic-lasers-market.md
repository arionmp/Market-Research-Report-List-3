<p><p>Excimer lasers are a type of ophthalmic laser used for refractive eye surgeries such as LASIK. They work by emitting ultraviolet light to reshape the cornea and correct vision problems like nearsightedness, farsightedness, and astigmatism. </p><p>On the other hand, femtosecond ophthalmic lasers are cutting-edge technology used for creating precise incisions in the eye during surgeries like cataract removal and corneal transplants. They emit ultrashort pulses of light to create very fine and accurate cuts, resulting in improved surgical outcomes and faster healing times.</p></p>
