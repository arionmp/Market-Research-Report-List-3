<p><p>Diabetes Mellitus drugs market consists of two main types: Oral medicine and Injection medicine. Oral medicines are taken by mouth and help regulate blood sugar levels by increasing insulin production or improving insulin sensitivity. Injection medicines are taken by injecting them into the body, usually through a needle or a syringe. These medications are often used when oral medicines are not effective in controlling blood sugar levels. Both types of medications play a crucial role in managing diabetes and preventing complications associated with the disease.</p></p>
