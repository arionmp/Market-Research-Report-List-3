<p><p>Safety prefilled syringes market is segmented into two main types: Glass Prefilled Syringes and Polymer Prefilled Syringes. Glass Prefilled Syringes are made from borosilicate glass, providing excellent chemical resistance and visibility. They are commonly used for biologics and vaccines. Polymer Prefilled Syringes, on the other hand, are made from plastic materials such as cyclic olefin polymers or copolymers. They offer advantages such as reduced breakage risk and compatibility with a wider range of drugs. Both types play a significant role in the growth of the prefilled syringes market.</p></p>
