<p><p>Between Bearing (BB) pumps are categorized into different types based on the number of bearings supporting the shaft. BB1 pumps have a single bearing at each end, whereas BB2 pumps have two bearings. BB3 pumps have three bearings, with two at one end and one at the other. BB4 pumps have four bearings evenly distributed along the shaft, while BB5 pumps have five bearings. Each type offers different levels of support and operational efficiency based on the specific application requirements.</p></p>
