<p><p>Microbiology Electropermeabilization Technology Market includes three main types of pulses used for cell membrane permeabilization: Exponential Decay Pulse, Square Wave Pulse, and Time Constant Pulse. Exponential Decay Pulse is characterized by a rapid increase in pulse intensity followed by a gradual decrease. Square Wave Pulse delivers a consistent pulse intensity over a set period. Time Constant Pulse delivers a pulse with a fixed duration, allowing for precise control of the permeabilization process. Each type offers unique advantages for various microbiology applications.</p></p>
