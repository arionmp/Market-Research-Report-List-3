<p><p>Protective packaging for shipping comes in three main market types: active systems, passive systems, and hybrid systems. Active systems use technology like temperature-controlled containers or shock sensors to actively protect the shipment during transit. Passive systems use materials like bubble wrap or foam inserts to provide a physical barrier against damage. Hybrid systems combine elements of both active and passive systems to provide comprehensive protection during shipping. Each system type addresses different challenges in protecting products during transportation.</p></p>
