<p><p>Meat stabilizer blends are commonly classified into two types: plant-based and animal-based. Plant-based meat stabilizer blends are derived from ingredients such as seaweed, guar gum, or other plant sources to enhance the texture, stability, and shelf life of plant-based meat products. On the other hand, animal-based meat stabilizer blends are made from proteins and other animal-derived compounds to provide similar functions for conventional meat products. Both types play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and consistency of meat products.</p></p>
