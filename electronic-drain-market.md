<p><p>The Electronic Drain market consists of two main types: Automatic Drain and Manual Drain. Automatic Drains are designed to automatically release condensate from air compressors and other systems without requiring manual intervention, providing convenience and efficiency. On the other hand, Manual Drains require manual operation to release condensate, offering a more cost-effective option for smaller systems or applications where automation is not necessary.
