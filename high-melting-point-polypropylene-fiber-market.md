<p><p>High melting point polypropylene fiber is categorized into three types based on its melt flow rate: -20-30 g/10 min, 30.1-35 g/10 min, and 35.1-100 g/10 min. The -20-30 g/10 min type offers a balance between strength and flexibility, making it suitable for various applications. The 30.1-35 g/10 min type provides enhanced flexibility and softness, ideal for textiles and clothing. The 35.1-100 g/10 min type is more cost-effective and easier to process, making it suitable for non-woven fabrics and packaging materials.</p></p>
