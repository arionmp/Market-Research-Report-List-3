<p><p>Retinoscopes are primarily classified into two types based on their design and usage: Hand Held Type and Table Top Type. Hand Held Type retinoscopes are compact and portable devices that are manually operated by the user for quick and convenient eye examinations. Table Top Type retinoscopes are larger stationary devices that are often used in clinical settings for more detailed and accurate eye examinations. Both types of retinoscopes play a crucial role in diagnosing refractive errors and determining the appropriate prescription for eyeglasses or contact lenses.</p></p>
