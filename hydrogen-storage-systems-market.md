<p><p>Hydrogen storage systems can be categorized into three main types: High-Pressure Hydrogen Storage Systems, Cryogenic Liquid Hydrogen Storage Systems, and Other systems. High-Pressure systems involve storing hydrogen gas in tanks at high pressure, while Cryogenic systems store liquid hydrogen at extremely low temperatures. Other systems may include materials-based storage, such as metal hydrides or carbon-based materials, as well as chemical storage methods. Each type of storage system has its own advantages and limitations, depending on factors such as cost, efficiency, and safety.</p></p>
