<p><p>Beverage carriers come in two main types - semi-rigid and rigid. Semi-rigid carriers are typically made of materials like cardboard or thin plastic, providing some flexibility while still offering protection for the beverages inside. Rigid carriers, on the other hand, are usually made of sturdier materials like wood or thick plastic, providing a more secure and durable option for transporting beverages. Both types cater to different consumer needs, with semi-rigid being more lightweight and portable, while rigid offers more strength and durability.</p></p>
