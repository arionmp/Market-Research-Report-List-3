<p><p>Automotive lamps market encompasses three main types of lighting technologies: Halogen lighting, HID lighting, and LED lighting. Halogen lighting, the traditional choice, offers cost-effective illumination. HID lighting, known for its brightness and energy efficiency, emits light through an electric arc. LED lighting, the most advanced option, is highly energy efficient and durable. Each type of lighting technology caters to different consumer needs and preferences in terms of brightness, energy efficiency, longevity, and cost-effectiveness in the automotive industry.</p></p>
