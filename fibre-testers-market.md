<p><p>Fibre testers market types include a variety of equipment used to measure different properties of fibres. Tensile testers assess the strength and elasticity of fibres, while abrasion testers evaluate their resistance to wear and tear. Color fastness testers determine how well the colour of a fibre holds up over time, and air permeability testers measure how easily air can pass through a material. Waterproofness testers assess the ability of fibres to repel water, and there are other testers available for different specific properties of fibres.</p></p>
