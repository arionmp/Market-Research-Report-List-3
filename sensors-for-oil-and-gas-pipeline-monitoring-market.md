<p><p>Sensors play a crucial role in oil and gas pipeline monitoring by detecting various parameters. The Hall Sensor type uses a magnetic field to measure flow rates and detect leaks. Fiber Optic Sensor technology utilizes light signals to monitor temperature, pressure, and vibrations along pipelines. Pressure Sensors are used to measure the pressure inside the pipelines to ensure safe operations. Other sensor types used include ultrasonic and acoustic sensors that detect leaks and corrosion in pipelines, ensuring the integrity and efficiency of the oil and gas transportation system.</p></p>
