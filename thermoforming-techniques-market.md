<p><p>Thermoforming Techniques Market types consist of Pressure Forming, Vacuum Forming, Matched Mold Forming, Twin Sheet Forming, and Mechanical Forming. Pressure Forming involves using air pressure to force the material against a mold. Vacuum Forming uses suction to shape the material around a mold. Matched Mold Forming uses two molds to shape the material. Twin Sheet Forming fuses two sheets together to form a double-walled product. Mechanical Forming involves using mechanical force to shape the material. Each technique offers unique advantages and is used in various industries.</p></p>
