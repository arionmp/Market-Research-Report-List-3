<p><p>Cold storage sandwich panels are insulated panels used in the construction of cold storage facilities such as warehouses, walk-in freezers, and refrigerated buildings. These panels consist of two outer layers of metal or other durable material with an insulated core in between, providing excellent thermal insulation properties. They help maintain a consistent temperature inside the cold storage facility, reducing energy consumption and keeping goods fresh for a longer period.</p><p>The global cold storage sandwich panels market is expected to witness significant growth over the forecast period. The increasing demand for cold storage facilities in the food & beverage industry, pharmaceutical industry, and other sectors is driving the market growth.
