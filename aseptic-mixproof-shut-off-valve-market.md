<p><p>Aseptic Mixproof Shut-off Valves come in various types, including Bearing Shut-off Valve, Diaphragm Shut-off Valve, and others. Diaphragm Shut-off Valves utilize a flexible membrane to regulate the flow, ensuring a tight seal and preventing contamination.
