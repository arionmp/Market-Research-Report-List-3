<p><p>Automotive Engine Oil Pump market consists of two main types - Mechanical Oil Pump and Electric Oil Pump. Mechanical Oil Pump, driven by the engine, circulates oil through the engine components to lubricate and reduce friction. On the other hand, Electric Oil Pump utilizes an electric motor to perform the same function. Mechanical Oil Pump is commonly found in traditional internal combustion engines, while Electric Oil Pump is becoming increasingly popular in hybrid and electric vehicles due to their efficiency and flexibility in oil circulation.</p></p>
