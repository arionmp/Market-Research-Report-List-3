Polyamide yarn, also known as nylon yarn, is known for its high strength, durability, and resistance to abrasion, making it suitable for high-performance tires. On the other hand, polyester yarn is valued for its excellent dimensional stability and low moisture absorption, which also makes it a popular choice for tire manufacturing. Both types of yarn play a crucial role in ensuring the quality and performance of high-performance tires.</p></p>
