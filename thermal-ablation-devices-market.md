<p><p>Thermal ablation devices market consists of various types such as interstitial probes, grounded probes, needle applications, radiofrequency ablation, hydrothermal ablation, and microwave ablation. Interstitial probes are used for internal tissue ablation, grounded probes for minimizing tissue damage, and needle applications for targeted therapy. Radiofrequency ablation utilizes heat to destroy tissue, hydrothermal ablation uses hot water to ablate tissue, and microwave ablation uses electromagnetic waves. These devices are increasingly being used for minimally invasive treatments of tumors and other medical conditions.</p></p>
