<p><p>Cooled Thermal Infrared Detector market consists of two main types of detectors, Vanadium Oxide (VOx) and Amorphous Silicon (A-Si). VOx detectors offer high sensitivity and resolution, making them suitable for demanding applications such as military and defense. On the other hand, A-Si detectors are cost-effective and more suited for commercial applications. Both types of detectors require cooling to operate efficiently and provide accurate thermal imaging capabilities. The choice between VOx and A-Si depends on the specific requirements of the application.</p></p>
