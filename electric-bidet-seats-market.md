<p><p>Electric bidet seats come in three main types: tank, tankless, and hybrid. Tank models have a reservoir that holds and heats water, providing a consistent temperature during use. Tankless bidet seats heat water on demand, eliminating the need for a reservoir and offering unlimited warm water. Hybrid bidet seats combine features of both tank and tankless models, providing a balance of consistent water temperature and energy efficiency. Each type offers unique benefits and features, catering to different preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
