<p><p>Underfloor heating is a popular heating option for residential buildings that provides efficient and comfortable warmth. There are two main types of underfloor heating systems: Hydronic Heating and Electric Heating. </p><p>Hydronic Heating systems use heated water to warm the floors and the surrounding space. These systems are usually more energy-efficient but require a boiler for operation. </p><p>Electric Heating systems use electric cables or mats under the floor to generate heat. These systems are easier to install and control, but may be more expensive to operate.</p></p>
