<p><strong>The Stimulation Materials Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Proppants</li><li>Chemicals</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The stimulation materials market consists primarily of two key segments: proppants and chemicals. Proppants are granular materials, typically sand or ceramic beads, used to prop open fractures created in the rock during hydraulic fracturing. These materials help to maintain the flow of oil and gas from the well to the surface. Chemicals, on the other hand, are used to enhance the efficiency of the fracturing process by reducing friction, preventing corrosion, and improving fluid characteristics. Both segments play crucial roles in ensuring the success of stimulation operations in the oil and gas industry.</p></p>
