Two common types of pre-printed wire labels are self-laminating wire labels and heat shrink wire labels. Self-laminating wire labels provide protection from water and abrasion, while heat shrink wire labels offer a more permanent solution by shrinking to conform to the wire when heat is applied. Both types are essential for organization and proper identification of wires in various applications.</p></p>
