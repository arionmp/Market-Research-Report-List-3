<p><strong>The Automotive TPMS（Tire-Pressure Monitoring System) Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct TPMS</li><li>Indirect TPMS</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Automotive TPMS (Tire-Pressure Monitoring System) market includes two types: Direct TPMS and Indirect TPMS. </p><p>- Direct TPMS uses sensors in each tire to directly measure tire pressure and transmit data to the driver. This system offers real-time and accurate monitoring of tire pressure.</p><p>- Indirect TPMS, on the other hand, calculates tire pressure based on wheel speed and other vehicle data. While less expensive than Direct TPMS, Indirect TPMS is typically less accurate and may require calibration.</p></p>
