<p><p>Polyphenylene Sulfone Resins are available in two main types in the market, Powder PPSU and Granular PPSU. Powder PPSU is finely ground PPSU resin that is typically used for powder coating applications. Granular PPSU, on the other hand, consists of small pellets of PPSU resin that are suitable for injection molding and extrusion processes. Both types offer excellent heat resistance, chemical resistance, and high mechanical strength, making them suitable for a wide range of industrial applications.</p></p>
