<p><p>Full Body Scanners can be classified into two main types: X-ray Scanner and Millimeter Wave Scanner market. X-ray Scanners use low levels of X-ray radiation to create detailed images of the body, allowing security personnel to detect hidden items on a person's body. Millimeter Wave Scanners use radio waves to create a three-dimensional image of the body, providing a non-invasive way to detect concealed objects. Both types have gained popularity in security checkpoints at airports, government buildings, and other high-security areas.</p></p>
