<p><p>PMMA, or polymethyl methacrylate, engineering plastics are available in three main types: General PMMA, Heat Resistant PMMA, and Impact Resistant PMMA. General PMMA is widely used for its optical clarity and easy processing capabilities. Heat Resistant PMMA is designed to withstand high temperatures without losing its structural integrity, making it ideal for applications requiring heat resistance. Impact Resistant PMMA is specifically formulated to absorb impact energy, providing enhanced durability and strength in demanding environments. Each type of PMMA serves unique purposes in various industries.</p></p>
