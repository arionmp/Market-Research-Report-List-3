<p><strong>The Genotyping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polymerase Chain Reaction</li><li>Sequencing</li><li>Microarray</li><li>Electrophoresis</li><li>MALDI-TOF</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Genotyping market encompasses various technologies used for identifying genetic variations among individuals. Polymerase Chain Reaction (PCR) is a widely used method that amplifies DNA sequences for analysis. Sequencing involves determining the exact sequence of nucleotides in a DNA sample. Microarray technology allows for the simultaneous analysis of thousands of genetic variations. Electrophoresis separates DNA fragments based on size. MALDI-TOF is a mass spectrometry technique used for genotyping single nucleotide polymorphisms. Each of these methods plays a crucial role in genetic research and personalized medicine.</p></p>
