Aryl Aminoalcohol Compounds work by targeting the parasite's cell membrane, Antifolate Compounds inhibit the synthesis of DNA and RNA in the parasite, and Artemisinin Compounds disrupt the parasite's cellular function. These different types of malaria drugs target various aspects of the parasite's biology, providing a range of options for treatment and prevention of malaria.</p></p>
