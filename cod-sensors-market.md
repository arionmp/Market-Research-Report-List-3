<p><p>COD sensors, or chemical oxygen demand sensors, come in two main types: digital and analog. Digital COD sensors provide precise and accurate measurements through digital signals, making them ideal for applications that require high levels of accuracy and reliability. Analog COD sensors, on the other hand, provide continuous measurements through analog signals, making them suitable for applications where real-time data monitoring is necessary. Both types of sensors play a crucial role in monitoring water quality and ensuring environmental safety.</p></p>
