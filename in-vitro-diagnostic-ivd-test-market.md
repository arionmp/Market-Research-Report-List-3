Molecular Diagnostics analyze DNA or RNA to detect genetic variations. Point of Care Testing (POCT) allows for rapid testing at the patient's bedside. Other markets may include hematology, coagulation, and urinalysis tests. Each type serves a specific purpose in diagnosing and monitoring various medical conditions.</p></p>
