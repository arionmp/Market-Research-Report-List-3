<p><p>Automobile steel plate spring market can be classified into two main types: Multi Leaf Spring and Little Leaf Spring. Multi Leaf Spring consists of several layers of steel plates stacked on top of each other, providing strong suspension for heavy-duty vehicles. On the other hand, Little Leaf Spring is a simpler design with fewer layers, suitable for smaller vehicles or lighter loads. Both types of steel plate springs play crucial roles in providing stability, support, and flexibility in a vehicle's suspension system.</p></p>
