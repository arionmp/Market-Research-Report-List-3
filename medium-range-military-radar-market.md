<p><p>Medium Range Military Radar Market Types include various components such as Antenna, Transmitter, Receiver, Duplexer, Display, Digital Signal Processor, and Stabilization System. The Antenna is responsible for transmitting and receiving radar signals, while the Transmitter generates the radar signal. The Receiver decodes the signals received, and the Duplexer switches the radar system between transmitting and receiving modes. The Display provides visual representation of the radar data, and the Digital Signal Processor processes the data for analysis. The Stabilization System ensures accuracy and stability of the radar system.</p></p>
