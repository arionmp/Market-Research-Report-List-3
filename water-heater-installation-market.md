<p><p>The water heater installation market can be divided into two main types: tank storage and tankless. Tank storage water heaters store and heat water in a large tank, providing a steady supply of hot water. On the other hand, tankless water heaters heat water on demand, eliminating the need for a storage tank and providing a more energy-efficient and space-saving option. Both types of water heaters have their own advantages and are popular choices based on consumer preferences and needs in the market.</p></p>
