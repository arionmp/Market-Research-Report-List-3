<p><p>Touchless car wash machines come in two main types: Tunnel Type and Rollover Type. Tunnel Type machines allow cars to drive through a tunnel-like structure where high-pressure sprayers and brushes clean the vehicle. Rollover Type machines, on the other hand, have a system that moves over the stationary car to perform the cleaning. Both types are popular in the market due to their efficiency and convenience in providing a thorough wash without the need for physical contact.</p></p>
