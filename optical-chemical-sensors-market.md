<p><strong>The Optical Chemical Sensors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Infrared Sensors</li><li>Fibre Optic Sensors</li><li>Photoionisation Sensors</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Optical chemical sensors are classified into different types based on their technology and application. Infrared sensors measure the absorption of infrared radiation by a specific chemical compound. Fibre optic sensors use optical fibres to detect chemical substances by measuring light intensity changes. Photoionisation sensors work by ionising molecules using ultraviolet light to detect trace amounts of chemicals. Other types include luminescence sensors and surface plasmon resonance sensors, each offering unique advantages for various chemical sensing applications.</p></p>
