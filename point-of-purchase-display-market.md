<p><p>Point-of-purchase displays come in various types depending on their placement and purpose in the retail environment. Floor displays are large standalone structures that attract attention from customers walking through aisles. Pallet displays are typically used in bulk stores to showcase products on pallets. End-cap displays are placed at the end of aisles to promote specific products. Counter top displays are placed near checkout counters to encourage impulse purchases. Other types of displays include hanging displays, side kick displays, and shelf displays. Each type serves a specific marketing purpose to drive sales.</p></p>
