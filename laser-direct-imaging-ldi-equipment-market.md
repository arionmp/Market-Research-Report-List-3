<p><p>Laser Direct Imaging (LDI) Equipment market includes two main types of equipment: Polygon Mirror 365nm and DMD 405nm systems. Polygon Mirror 365nm systems use a spinning mirror to direct the laser beam onto the surface to be imaged. On the other hand, DMD 405nm systems utilize Digital Micromirror Devices to reflect the laser light onto the imaging surface. Both types of equipment offer high precision and speed in the direct imaging process, catering to the varying needs of industries such as electronics manufacturing and PCB fabrication.</p></p>
