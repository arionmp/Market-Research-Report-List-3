<p><p>BT Substrate Market consists of various types such as WB BGA, WB CSP, and FC CSP. WB BGA (Wire Bond Ball Grid Array) substrate is widely used in high power applications due to its excellent thermal performance. WB CSP (Wire Bond Chip Scale Package) substrate offers a compact design for small electronic devices. FC CSP (Flip Chip Chip Scale Package) substrate provides superior electrical performance and high-density interconnections. Each type caters to different needs and requirements in the electronics industry, making them essential components in various applications.</p></p>
