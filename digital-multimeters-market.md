<p><strong>The Digital Multimeters Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Manual Range Digital Multimeters</li><li>Automatic Range Digital Multimeters</li><li>Automatic-Manual Range Digital Multimeters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Digital multimeters are available in three main types based on their range selection capabilities. Manual range digital multimeters require users to manually adjust the range settings for each measurement. Automatic range digital multimeters automatically select the appropriate range based on the measured signal, simplifying the measurement process. Automatic-manual range digital multimeters offer a combination of both manual and automatic range selection options, providing flexibility for users to choose the most suitable range for their specific measurement needs.</p></p>
