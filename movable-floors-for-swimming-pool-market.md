<p><p>Movable floors for swimming pool markets can be categorized into two main types: suspended type and floating type. Suspended type movable floors are designed to be installed below the water's surface and can be raised or lowered to adjust the pool's depth. Floating type movable floors are installed on the water's surface and can also be adjusted to change the pool's depth. Both types offer versatility and flexibility in pool design, making them popular choices for both residential and commercial swimming pools.</p></p>
