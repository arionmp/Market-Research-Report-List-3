<p><p>Polymer Dispersed Liquid Crystal (PDLC) Smart Film is available in two main types: None-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film and Self-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film. None-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film requires a separate adhesive to be applied to surfaces for installation, providing flexibility in application. Self-Adhesive PDLC Smart Film, on the other hand, comes with an adhesive backing for easy installation directly onto surfaces without the need for additional adhesives. Both types offer privacy and light control by transitioning from opaque to transparent states.</p></p>
