<p><p>Cable tie tools come in two main types - manual and pneumatic. Manual tools are operated by hand, requiring physical exertion to tighten and cut cable ties. They are generally more affordable and portable than pneumatic tools. Pneumatic tools, on the other hand, are powered by compressed air and offer faster and more efficient operation. They are often used in industries where large volumes of cable ties need to be installed quickly. Both types of tools cater to different needs and preferences in the market.</p></p>
