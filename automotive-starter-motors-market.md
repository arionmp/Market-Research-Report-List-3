<p><strong>The Automotive Starter Motors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Direct Control</li><li>Electromagnetic Control</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Automotive starter motors are primarily divided into two main market types: Direct Control and Electromagnetic Control. </p><p>Direct Control starter motors use a solenoid to engage the starter gear with the flywheel, providing a direct connection for starting the engine. This type of starter motor is commonly found in older vehicles.</p><p>Electromagnetic Control starter motors use an electromagnetic switch to engage the starter gear with the flywheel. This type of starter motor is more common in modern vehicles due to its efficiency and reliability.</p></p>
