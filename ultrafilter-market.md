<p><strong>The Ultrafilter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Tube Type</li><li>Roll Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ultrafilters can be classified into two main types: Tube Type and Roll Type. Tube Type ultrafilters are designed in a cylindrical shape with a membrane enclosed in a tube. They are commonly used for water purification and desalination processes. On the other hand, Roll Type ultrafilters consist of a membrane wound around a central core. These are ideal for industrial applications such as pharmaceutical and chemical industries. Both types offer efficient filtration and separation capabilities, catering to different market needs and requirements.</p></p>
