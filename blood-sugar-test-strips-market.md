<p><p>The Blood Sugar Test Strips market can be categorized into different types based on the enzymes used in the strips to measure blood glucose levels. The most common types are Glucose Oxidase and Glucose Dehydrogenase test strips. Glucose Oxidase strips use the enzyme glucose oxidase to catalyze the reaction between glucose and oxygen, producing a color change that indicates blood sugar levels. Glucose Dehydrogenase strips, on the other hand, use the enzyme glucose dehydrogenase to measure blood glucose levels. Other types of test strips may utilize different enzymes or technologies for blood sugar testing.</p></p>
