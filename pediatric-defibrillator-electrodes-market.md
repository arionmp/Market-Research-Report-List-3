<p><p>Pediatric defibrillator electrodes market includes internal and external options. Internal pediatric defibrillator electrodes are implanted inside the body and are connected to the defibrillator device. They deliver electric shocks directly to the heart. External pediatric defibrillator electrodes are placed on the chest and deliver electric shocks through the skin to the heart during emergency situations. Both types play a crucial role in treating children with cardiac arrest and other life-threatening conditions.</p></p>
