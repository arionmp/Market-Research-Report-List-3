<p><p>Rotary screen changer is available in two main market types: single piston and double piston. Single piston rotary screen changers have a single hydraulic piston to initiate the screen change process, making them simpler and more cost-effective for smaller applications. On the other hand, double piston rotary screen changers feature two hydraulic pistons, allowing for quicker and more efficient screen changes, making them ideal for high-volume production environments. Both types offer benefits depending on the specific needs and scale of the operation.</p></p>
