<p><p>Miniature Thermopile Detectors come in two main types: Circular Window Format and Square Window Format. The Circular Window Format is characterized by a circular window opening, which allows for a wider field of view and more evenly distributed incoming radiation. On the other hand, the Square Window Format features a square window opening, which offers a more focused detection area. Both formats cater to different application needs and preferences, providing versatility and flexibility in thermal sensing technologies.</p></p>
