<p><p>Hepatitis B testing market includes tests for the virus itself, antibodies produced by the body to fight the virus, and antigens present on the surface of the virus. Virus testing directly detects the presence of the hepatitis B virus in the bloodstream. Antibody testing identifies whether the body has developed immunity to the virus. Antigen testing detects proteins on the surface of the virus, helping to diagnose active infection. These different types of tests play a crucial role in diagnosing and managing hepatitis B infection.</p></p>
