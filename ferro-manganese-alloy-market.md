<p><p>Ferro Manganese Alloy Market is categorized into three types based on carbon content. High carbon ferro manganese contains around 70-80% manganese and 6-7% carbon, used in steel production for deoxidization and desulfurization. Medium carbon ferro manganese has 1-2% carbon and 65-70% manganese, suitable for manufacturing steel and cast iron. Low carbon ferro manganese consists of 0.5% carbon and around 85% manganese, preferred for producing high-strength low-alloy steel. Each type of ferro manganese alloy serves specific purposes in various industrial applications.</p></p>
