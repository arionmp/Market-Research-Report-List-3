<p><p>Launch and Recovery System market consists of three main types: Crane LARS, Ramp Style LARS, and Others. Crane LARS involve using a crane to lift equipment in and out of the water, typically used for smaller vessels. Ramp Style LARS use a ramp that can be lowered into the water for easy access to launch and recover equipment, often used for larger vessels. Other types of LARS include winch-based systems and A-frames, offering different methods for launching and recovering equipment.</p></p>
