<p><p>Reverse Osmosis (RO) Artificial Membrane market types include Cellulose-based Membrane and Thin film Composite Membrane. Cellulose-based membranes are made from natural materials and are cost-effective, but have lower rejection rates. Thin film composite membranes, on the other hand, are synthetic membranes with higher rejection rates and are more durable, but also more expensive. Both types of membranes are widely used in RO systems for various applications such as water purification, wastewater treatment, and desalination.</p></p>
