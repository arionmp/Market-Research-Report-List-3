<p><strong>The Shaft Impactors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Horizontal Shaft Impactors</li><li>Vertical Shaft Impactors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Horizontal Shaft Impactors (HSI) are utilized for primary or secondary crushing applications and operate by propelling material against a solid surface. They are typically used in quarries and mining operations. On the other hand, Vertical Shaft Impactors (VSI) are commonly used in the production of high-quality manufactured sand and premium shaped aggregates. They operate by throwing the material against a fixed surface, resulting in shaping and grading it. Both types of shaft impactors play a crucial role in the crushing and processing industry.</p></p>
